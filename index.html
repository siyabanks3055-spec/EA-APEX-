  4. Download the APK file to your phone
  5. Open the APK and tap "Install"
  6. Done! App will appear on your home screen

Method 2: Using APKMaker

  1. Go to: app.webtoapp.tech
  2. Enter app URL: https://siyabanks3055-spec.github.io/EA-APEX-/
  3. Add app name: EA APEX
  4. Download APK to your Android device
  5. Enable "Unknown Sources"** in Settings
  6. Install the APK

⚠️ Important: Enable Installation from Unknown Sources

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll to Security
  3. Find "Unknown Sources" or "Install from Unknown Sources"
  4. Toggle it ON
  5. Now you can install APK files

🍎 iPhone/iPad Installation

  1. Open Safari browser on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Go to: https://siyabanks3055-spec.github.io/EA-APEX-/
  3. Wait for the app to fully load
  4. Tap the Share button (bottom center, arrow icon)
  5. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  6. Name it: "EA APEX" (or your preferred name)
  7. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
  8. Done! App now appears on your home screen
